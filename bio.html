Ksenia Vasileva

Musician, bass player, performer, sound designer

Hello, dear visitor.

I'm Ksenia.

I was born in Saint Petersburg, Russia. As a professional bass player I was touring Europe and Russia for about 10 years. At the same time received a degree in Public Relations. I have reached my goals back at home and decided to change my life, that's how I ended up being at Berklee. I graduated in Summer 2021 with double major degree: Electronic Production, Design and Performance.

I have 2 favorite states - being an investigator and being a creator.

The investigator in me loves to travel, to be outside the comfort zone and to collect impressions and experiences. I love extremes - from hitchhiking and sleeping on the streets to staying in 5 star hotels and playing music for the presidents. I'm always starving to fill my imaginary list with new impressions.

The other part of myself is tormented by the desire to invent something. And my Berklee experience happened to be a turning point. I used musical instruments before. Now I want to invent instruments, and Berklee gave me resources for being able to do that.

These two states for me are lovebirds. The more experiences I have in my bag, the more inspiration and imagination I have for creating and inventing.

Looking forward to new amazing oportunities.

Making this website.

Keeping my mind and heart open.
